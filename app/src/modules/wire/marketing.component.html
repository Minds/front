Reward your fans

Build custom reward tiers for your fans to subscribe to your products and services, in exchange for points dollars or Bitcoin.

Wire is a peer-to-peer system for channels to send each other dollars, points or Bitcoin as a tip or in exchange for rewards, exclusive content and services. Various reward tiers can be set with custom offerings, and you can choose to support others on a one-time or monthly basis.

You will see a Wire icon (lightning bolt) on channels and individual posts where you can send dollars, points or Bitcoin. You will also be able to see how much each individual post has earned, as well as how many views it has received, directly underneath the center of the post.

Reward tiers describe what a supporter will get in exchange for a specific amount of dollars, points or Bitcoin. If you Wire someone the amount they set to unlock a reward tier or more, you will gain access to all of the rewards in that tier. You will also gain access to all of the rewards below that tier.

Once you have monetized your channel, you can create and edit your custom reward tiers on your channel page, under the “Rewards” section. Here, you will be able to set the cost of each reward tier, along with a description of what is included in that tier.

Our reward tiers are customizable. You are able to set the price and description of each reward tier on your channel page. The description should explain what a supporter will receive once they have Wired you the necessary amount (ie. exclusive content, a one-on-one dinner, a shout-out in a video, etc.).

Select the Wire lightning bolt icon on the bottom right-hand corner of your post bar to select which of your reward tiers will be able to access the post. You can type in any amount at or above the numbers stated in your tiers as well. For instance, you could set an individual post to cost $100 to access, even if your rewards only go up to $50. Subscribers who do not qualify for the selected reward tier will see a custom paywall and will need to Wire you the remaining amount required to view the post.

In the Wire section of your Wallet, you will be able to track how many Wires you have received, how much you have earned in total, and what your average Wire amount is. You will also be able to see which specific users have sent you money, including the date and amount of each individual Wire.

If you have chosen to Wire another channel on a recurring monthly basis, you can cancel your subscription in your settings, under the billing section.

In the Wire section of your Wallet, click on the 'Options' button to create a custom preview that will be displayed to all users who have not yet Wired you enough to unlock the reward tiers that are defined on your channel.

You will be paid out on monthly basis. Please note that monetized users agree to our Terms and Conditions and are subject to risks from chargebacks or refunds.

There are certain fees that apply when a Wire transaction occurs.

Payment Processing Fees
  • Stripe handles all payment processing for Minds
  • Stripe charges a fee of $0.30 + 2.9% on all credit card transactions
  • Learn more about Stripe's fees here
Service Fees
  • Minds charges a 4% fee on the net amount of the transaction once the payment processing fees have been deducted
  • This fee is necessary to cover application, service and infrastructure costs
Example
  • You receive a $10 payment on Minds through Wire
  • Stripe takes 2.9% + $0.30 of the $10
    • Stripe Fee = (10 x 0.029) + $0.30 = $0.59
  • Minds takes 4% of the original charge ($10) minus the Stripe fee ($0.59)
    • Minds Fee = (10 - 0.59) x .04 = $0.3764
  • You receive $9.03 in your account
