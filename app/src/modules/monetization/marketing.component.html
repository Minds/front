Minds believes you should be rewarded for your efforts online. Monetizing your channel activates the following features that enable you to generate revenue on Minds:

  • Affiliate Program
  • Exclusive Content
  • Wire
  • Ad-Revenue Share
  • Peer-to-Peer Boost

By joining the Affiliate Program, you will earn 25% of the revenue that Minds generates from users that sign up through your referral link. The products that qualify for the Affiliate Program include:

  • Point Purchases
  • Paid Boost (coming soon)
  • Priority Boost (coming soon)
  • Minds Plus (coming soon)

Monitor the revenue you have earned through the Affiliate Program in your Wallet. Payment processing fees will apply to all transactions and will be deducted prior to revenue distribution.

Earn subscription revenue on Minds by creating exclusive content that your fans pay to access. Set a monthly subscription price in your Wallet and select the '$' sign on your content to make it exclusive. These posts will display a custom paywall that prompts your fans to become monthly supporters.

You will notice a lightning bolt icon below all of your posts. This enables you to directly receive points, dollars, or Bitcoin from other channels as a reward for your quality content.

Send offers of points, dollars or Bitcoin to other channels in exchange for a guaranteed Remind (share) of your content with their audience. This enables you to target your boosted content to new, like-minded audiences outside of your own subscribers.

Receive offers of points, dollars, or Bitcoin from other channels in exchange for a guaranteed Remind (share) of their content to your audience. You may optionally connect your Facebook page (and other third party sites in the future) to your account so that others can access your audience on other social media networks additionally.
