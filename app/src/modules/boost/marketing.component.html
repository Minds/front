Boost is the system we use to promote your content to a specific part of the Minds network so you can get more views and reach new audiences. Boost your content across the Minds network in exchange for points, USD or Bitcoin.1 point = 1 view. You can also choose to pay directly with dollars or Bitcoin for views when you post, at a rate of $1 per 1,000 views.

Click “Boost” after you post, and you will be prompted to give us some pieces of information, such as:

There are four different types of Boost:

  • Newsfeed
  • Sidebar
  • Channel
  • Priority

A Newsfeed Boost delivers guaranteed views on your posts in exchange for points, dollars, or Bitcoin. We place Newsfeed Boosts either in the rotating queue at the top of the newsfeed, or once every twelve posts as you scroll down the newsfeed.

A Sidebar Boost delivers guaranteed views on your channel, video, image or blog in exchange for dollars, points, or Bitcoin. We place Sidebar Boosts on sidebars across Minds for all channels to see. Sidebar views are not counted on the view counter.

A Channel Boost is a peer to peer offer to pay another Minds channel in dollars, points, or Bitcoin in exchange for a guaranteed Remind (share). Channel Boost offers may be accepted or rejected. Use a Channel Boost to target specific communities with content.

Build a great channel on Minds and earn revenue by accepting Channel Boost offers to Remind (share) other users’ content to your audience.

A Priority Boost places your content at the very front of the line. This enables you to fulfil the number of views you request much more quickly and react to breaking or live events. Priority Boost rates are USD only and subject to surge pricing. Minimum price is twice regular Boost rates.

A view is defined by the post passing over the center of your screen. Newsfeed views are counted for Newsfeed Boost only. Sidebar Boosts do not record a view, as they post to the left or right column.

Use categories to target your Boost to Minds channels who have listed their categories of interest in their Settings. When you Boost to a category, the post will be placed in front of users who have already opted-in to the category you selected. This helps ensure that your post is seen by the users who would be the most interested and likely to engage.

Target specific communities or channel networks with a Channel Boost, Channel Boosts are peer to peer offers for a guaranteed Remind to a Channels network.

There are two different ways to buy and pay for a Boost. The first is to buy points at https://www.minds.com/wallet/purchase. The points can then be exchanged for views at 1 point = 1 view.

The other way to buy a Boost is to pay directly when you select ‘Boost’ on your post. In the Boost prompt, you have the option to pay for views using your points, or by entering your credit card information.

The point reward system is currently the following:

  • Post a text/link activity = 1 point
  • Receive a comment = 5 points
  • Gain a subscriber = 5 points
  • Receive an upvote = 5 points
  • Get reminded = 5 points
  • Post a rich media activity = 15 points
  • Publish a blog = 15 points
  • Hourly check-in = 50 points

You can keep track of your boosted content in the Boost section of your Wallet. This can be accessed by selecting the Wallet (bank) icon in the top right corner of your browser, or by clicking the “Boost” button in the top right of your browser. In this console, you can keep track of the date and status of all of your Boosted content.
